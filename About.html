ABOUT

For everyone...

Hello, my name is Junho Lee.
I know a lot of people are suffering and scared due to the COVID-19 (Coronavirus).
Also, I heard a lot of shocking and desperate news such as many people getting rejected from hospitals.
Unfortunately, there are not many places in the US you can go to seek treatment for this virus due to the lack of
equipment and facilities.

Since I am not on the frontlines alongside doctors, nurses, or any other essential medical workers
who are fighting against covid-19 I can't help you directly.
However, I still wanted to help people, so I decided to create this website/application.
With this, I wish people can be better prepared and know where to go to when they start
to experience symptoms of the coronavirus at least.
"COVID-19 Testing Locations Map" allows you to find a testing location for the coronavirus very quickly.

I will keep everything such as the UI in this website as simple as possible so everyone can use it directly without any difficulty.

Thank you and stay healthy!
